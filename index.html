iCoders is a tech club of Dr. B. C. Roy Engineering College, Durgapur. Like clubs of other colleges, this club consists of members from every department who are interested in technologies and coding. This club is the first technical club in BCREC.

Key functions of iCoders will be to have

-> Geeky Sessions : There will be regular sessions on technologies and programming apart from our syllabus (Ex:- Reconfigurable Computing, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Cryptology, etc) by Members of the club, Teachers, Professionals. We will give priority to Open Source Technologies.
-> Coding : There will be regular online coding competitions for members as well as for the whole college. This will help students to become good programmers. And we will also have regular practice sessions among members, online as well as offline.
-> Events : Our best members of the club will represent our college in Tech Events, Open Source, Hackathon, etc. We will also make teams and participate in some big Online Tech Events & Competitions like the Microsoft Imagine Cup, Google Summer of Code and etc.
-> Projects : Our members will be involved in some Innovative Projects like creating apps for different Operating Systems, Web apps and etc. as teamwork under the guidance of our teacher's skills (Basically we will focus on 2 projects per year one in the summer break and one in the winter break, but we will make some project as well in between semester as well).
